# Privacy Policy — IGLens

_Last updated: 2026-06-21_

IGLens is a browser extension that runs entirely on your own device.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** IGLens has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. The developer
never receives any of your data.

## What the extension stores (locally)

To function, IGLens saves the following in your browser's local storage
(`chrome.storage.local`) on your device only:

- Your Instagram username and numeric user id (to make requests)
- The followers / following lists you load or import
- Your "keep" list and extension settings

This data never leaves your browser. Clearing it is one click away
(**Settings → Clear cached data**) or by removing the extension.

## How the extension works

When you click **Sync**, IGLens injects a script into your already-open
instagram.com tab and uses **your own logged-in session** to read your
followers/following and to remove or unfollow accounts you choose. All requests
go directly from your browser to Instagram — never through any third party.

## Permissions

- `instagram.com` host access — to read your lists and perform actions you initiate.
- `scripting` — to inject the agent into your Instagram tab.
- `storage` / `unlimitedStorage` — to cache your lists and settings locally.
- `sidePanel` — to show the user interface.

## Third parties

None. IGLens does not share data with anyone.
